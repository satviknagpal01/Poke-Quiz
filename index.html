\ Poke-Quiz
PokeDex

Arbok

Arbok is a serpentine Pokémon that resembles a cobra. It has narrow eyes and several sharp teeth.

Blastoise

Blastoise is a large, bipedal turtle Pokémon. Its body is blue and is mostly hidden by its tough, brown shell.

Bulbasaur

While it is young, it uses the nutrients that are stored in the seed on its back in order to grow.

Butterfree

Butterfree is a lepidopteran insect Pokémon which resembles a vaguely anthropomorphic butterfly with a purple body.

Caterpie

Its short feet are tipped with suction pads that enable it to tirelessly climb slopes and walls

Charizard

Its wings can carry this Pokémon close to an altitude of 4,600 feet. It blows out fire at very high temperatures.

Charmander

From the time it is born, a flame burns at the tip of its tail. Its life would end if the flame were to go out. It has a preference for hot things.

Charmeleon

Charmeleon is a bipedal, reptilian Pokémon. It has dark red scales and a cream underside from the chest down.

Clefairy

Its adorable behavior and cry make it highly popular. However, this cute Pokémon is rarely found.

Ekans

Ekans is a Poison-type Pokémon

Ivysaur

vysaur is a Grass & Poison Pokémon which evolves from Bulbasaur. It is vulnerable to Flying, Fire, Psychic and Ice moves.

Jigglypuff

By freely changing the wavelength of its voice, Jigglypuff sings a mysterious melody sure to make any listener sleepy

Pidgeotto

Pidgeotto is a raptor-like avian Pokémon. It is covered with brown feathers, has a cream-colored face, underside, and flight feathers.

Pikachu

Pikachu is a short, chubby rodent Pokémon. It is covered in yellow fur with two horizontal brown stripes on its back.

Rattata

Rattata is a small, quadrupedal rodent Pokémon. It has purple fur with a cream-colored face, paws, and underbelly.
